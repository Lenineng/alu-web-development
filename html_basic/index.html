President Kagame in Switzerland.

President Paul Kagame has attended the World Economic Forum (WEF) held in Davos, Switzerland from January 15th to 19th, 2024.

Section 1: Meet with Antony J. Blinken.

Here, President Paul Kagame met with Antony J. Blinken, who is in charge of foreign affairs of the United States of America, and discussed the long-standing insecurity in the eastern part of the Democratic Republic of the Congo..

Subsection 1.1: MEET WITH Jean Todt

President Kagame and Jean Todt discussed the development of transportation.

Section 2: Meet with Makhtar Diop

President Kagame talked to Makhtar Diop, Director of the International Finance Corporation (IFC).

Subsection 2.1: Meet with President zelensky

President Paul Kagame held talks with his Ukrainian counterpart, Volodymyr Zelensky, discussing the long-standing war in this country and the efforts being made to resolve it.
